Moonshine Murder

It’s 1925. The small cabin deep in the San Juan Mountains is the only home seventeen-year-old Lenora Giovanni has ever known. But when her father dies from tainted moonshine, leaving her alone, she is forced into a life of danger.






The Knock Over

Winner 2021 Will Rogers Medallion Award

Rusty is saving every penny earned working as a bank teller to marry Lenora and get out of town. When threatened by mobsters from Denver to repay a former moonshining partner’s debt, not only Rusty’s but Lenora’s lives are placed in danger.






The Snatcher

When a young hootch detective is the only one who can pinpoint a killer, will a spy for the feds go undercover to keep him above ground?
